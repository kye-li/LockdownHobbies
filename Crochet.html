Crochet

Create some cute jumpers or hats to brighten up the winter!

There are some materials you will need to get started with this hobby. As a basic guide, here is what you will need:

  • Crochet hooks - size 5mm is commonly used, but hooks of size 4mm, 4.5mm and 6mm are also often used.
  • Wool or Yarn - The amount needed depends on the pattern, but its best to stock up on a few balls of wool or yarn to be used in little projects
  • Scissors - helpful for cutting the wool at the end of your project
  • Pattern - it may be useful to print off these patterns so you can mark which rows you have completed
